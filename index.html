Click the button below.

The goal of this study is to understand how people use search tools, specifically how they choose among many search results and what factors they rely on as they assess search results with respect to their needs.

Our domain of interest is data science - i.e., all assets used in data analytics from datasets to people. The results of this study are going to inform the design of search algorithms for data science domain.
